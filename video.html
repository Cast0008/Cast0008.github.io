How to Embed a Video into a Website

Step 1: Get your video

Go to YouTube and open the video you want to embed.

Step 2: Click the share button

Below the video, click the “Share” button and look at the options given.

Step 3: Click embed

From the options, click the “Embed” option.

Step 4: Copy the embed code

Copy the embed code provided by YouTube.

Step 5: Paste the embed code into your HTML

Open your HTML file and paste the embed code where you want the video to appear.
